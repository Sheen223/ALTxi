How It Works

Four steps to prove you're a better coach. Same players, your tactics, a completely different result.

01

Choose Your Match

Choose any upcoming or live FIFA World Cup match. You'll receive the exact starting XI the real coach selected — same players, same squad.

02

Build Your Game Plan

Rearrange the formation, reposition players, set pressing intensity, defensive line, tempo, and playing style. The squad is locked — your strategy isn't.

03

Experience the Match

As real match events stream in — goals, cards, corners — ALTXI reinterprets them through your tactical lens, creating an alternate reality in real time.

04

See Your Results

At full time, see how your version compares to reality. Get a coach rating, tactical breakdown, player ratings, and a narrative of how your decisions changed the match.
